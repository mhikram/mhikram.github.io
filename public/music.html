My music setup

Most of my hobby music activities are done on a iPad Pro with a SonicPort interface.

  • Garageband for recording and multi-tracking
    • My band mates use Audacity to record their own tracks
  • FinalTouch for final audio production
  • LumaFusion for editing videos
    • Emulsio to stabilize videos
  • BIAS FX as portable amp modeler and effects
  • Yamaha THR10 as “third amp” for home practice
    • I run the THR Editor on Linux using WINE
  • AKG K131Studio headphones as monitors
  • iReal Pro to document songs and generate skeleton backing tracks

My live setup is:

  • 2014 LP Studio guitar
  • Fractal Audio AX8 as amp modeler and effects
    • I run AX-8 Edit on Linux using WINE
  • Mission Engineering SP-1 expression pedal
  • Xvive guitar wireless system
  • Shure SE-215CL in-ear monitors
  • t.bone IEM 75 wireless solution
